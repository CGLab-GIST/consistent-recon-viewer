Overview

This material provides full-resolution of images of the paper figures. There are two types of comparisons. First, equal-time comparison with progressive photon mapping methods (Fig. 6 in the paper); SPPM [1] and CPPM [2] and second, equal-time comparison with Deep-Combiner [3] (Fig. 8).
